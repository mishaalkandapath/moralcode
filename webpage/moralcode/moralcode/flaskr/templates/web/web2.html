Our Mission

To inform consumers on their rights when they use a new device:

We provide a readability and privacy score for each device (Apple Watch, Google Nest, other Internet of Things devices) to help you make an informed choice!

What Do Our Numbers Mean?

Readability Score:
Scores how easy it is to read a terms of service.
We use the Flesch Reading Ease Score to tell you how hard a ToS is to read. While the Flesch Score ranges from 0 to 100, we simplify the scores, A being easy to read, and E being hard to read.
Privacy Score:
Scores how private and fair a service is. An AI summarizes terms of service, warranty agreement, or terms and conditions. Then, a panel of experts checks over the summary and provides a score from A to E. To read the summary, click on the box with the product of interest.
